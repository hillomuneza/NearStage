Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RIAPMS

General Questions

What is RIAPMS?

RIAPMS (Rwanda Internship & Academic Placement Management System) is a national platform that connects students seeking internships with companies offering placement opportunities. We help bridge the gap between education and employment by matching students with internships based on their field of study, skills, and location preferences.

Is RIAPMS free to use?

Yes, RIAPMS is completely free for both students and companies. The platform is funded by the government to support youth employment and skills development in Rwanda.

Who can use RIAPMS?

RIAPMS is available to:

  • Students enrolled in Rwandan universities and TVET institutions
  • Companies registered in Rwanda looking to hire interns
  • Educational institutions for tracking student placements

For Students

How do I register as a student?

Click on "Register" and select "Student." You'll need to provide your personal details, educational information (university, course, year), skills, and location. You can then upload your CV and start applying for internships.

Can I apply for internships outside my district?

Yes! You can apply for internships anywhere in Rwanda. Our platform allows you to filter by province and district, but you're not limited to your location. Many students choose internships in different areas for diverse experience.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our smart matching system considers:

  • Your field of study
  • Your skills and qualifications
  • Location preferences
  • Company requirements

You'll receive notifications about internships that match your profile.

What happens after I apply?

After applying, the company will review your application. You'll receive email notifications about:

  • Application received
  • Interview invitation
  • Acceptance or rejection

If accepted, the company will issue a placement letter through the platform.

Are internships paid?

Internship compensation varies by company. Some companies offer stipends while others provide experience only. Each internship listing shows details about compensation. We recommend discussing this with the company during the interview process.

For Companies

How do I register my company?

Click on "Register" and select "Company." You'll need to provide your business registration details, company information, and contact person. All companies are verified by administrators before becoming active on the platform.

How do I post an internship?

Once your company is verified, log in to your dashboard and click "Post Internship." Fill in the details including job title, description, requirements, location, duration, and whether compensation is offered. Submit for admin approval.

How long does verification take?

Company verification typically takes 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email once your company is approved. If you need expedited verification, contact our support team.

Can I issue placement letters through the platform?

Yes! Our platform allows you to generate and send digital placement letters directly to accepted students. This helps formalize the internship arrangement and provides students with official documentation.

Technical Support

What if I forgot my password?

Click "Login" and then "Forgot Password." Enter your email address and you'll receive a link to reset your password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

How do I update my profile?

Log in to your dashboard and navigate to "Profile Settings" where you can update your personal information, education details, skills, and upload a new CV.

Is the platform mobile-friendly?

Yes! RIAPMS works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. You can also install it as a PWA (Progressive Web App) for offline access and a native app-like experience.
