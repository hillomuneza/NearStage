Our Mission

Empowering Rwanda's youth through meaningful internship opportunities

Transforming Career Paths for Rwandan Students

NearStage exists to bridge the gap between academic education and professional employment.

Our mission is to create a seamless connection between students seeking practical experience and companies offering meaningful internship opportunities. We believe that every student in Rwanda deserves access to quality internships that align with their field of study, location preferences, and career aspirations.

Through innovative technology and strategic partnerships, we're building Rwanda's most reliable internship placement platform—one that benefits students, educational institutions, and businesses alike.
