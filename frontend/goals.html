Our Strategic Goals

Building a sustainable future for Rwanda's youth through internship excellence

Our Vision

To become the leading internship placement platform in Africa, connecting every Rwandan student with meaningful career opportunities that contribute to national development.

What We Aim For

Strategic Objectives

1

Expand National Coverage

Ensure every student in Rwanda—regardless of location—has access to quality internship opportunities. We're expanding to all 30 districts and partnering with universities across the country.

2

Industry Partnerships

Build strong relationships with 500+ companies across all sectors including technology, agriculture, healthcare, finance, and tourism to provide diverse internship opportunities.

3

Technology Innovation

Continuously improve our AI matching algorithm to ensure better student-company matches. Implement new features like skills assessment, virtual interviews, and progress tracking.

4

Placement Success Rate

Achieve a 90% successful placement rate by improving our matching system, providing career guidance, and ensuring companies meet their commitment to interns.

5

Student Support Services

Provide comprehensive support including resume building, interview preparation, and career counseling to help students succeed in their internships and future careers.

6

Data-Driven Decisions

Utilize analytics to understand internship trends, identify gaps, and make informed decisions that benefit students and employers alike.

Our Journey

Roadmap to Success

Phase 1 - 2024

Foundation

Launch the platform, establish core partnerships with 50 companies, and register 5,000 students across major universities.

Phase 2 - 2025

Expansion

Expand to all provinces, grow to 200+ companies, implement AI matching, and achieve 1,000 successful placements.

Phase 3 - 2026

Excellence

Reach 500+ company partners, achieve 90% placement success rate, launch mobile app, and expand to regional markets.

Phase 4 - 2027+

Pan-African Leadership

Become the leading internship platform in Africa, sharing our model with other countries and continuously innovating.
