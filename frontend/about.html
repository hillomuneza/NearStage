About NearStage

Connecting Rwanda's students with career opportunities since 2024

Who We Are

Our Story

Bridging Education and Employment

NearStage (NearStage - Professional Internship & Placement Management System) was established to address a critical challenge facing Rwanda's youth: the gap between academic education and practical work experience.

Founded in 2024, our platform was developed in response to the nation's need for a structured, efficient system that connects students with meaningful internship opportunities across all sectors and regions.

We believe that practical work experience is essential for preparing Rwanda's future workforce. By connecting students with industry leaders, we're helping to build a skilled, employable generation that will drive the country's Vision 2050 development goals.
