Important Notice

By accessing or using RIAPMS, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use our platform.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By registering as a student, company, or administrator on RIAPMS, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these Terms of Service and our Privacy Policy.

2. User Accounts

Account Registration

To use our platform, you must create an account. You agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete registration information
  • Maintain the security of your account credentials
  • Accept responsibility for all activities under your account
  • Notify us immediately of any unauthorized access

Account Types

Student Accounts: Available to students enrolled in Rwandan educational institutions seeking internship opportunities.

Company Accounts: Available to legally registered businesses in Rwanda seeking to hire interns.

Admin Accounts: Reserved for authorized RIAPMS administrators.

3. Student Responsibilities

As a student user, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information about your education, skills, and qualifications
  • Only apply for internships that match your field of study and skills
  • Respond professionally to company communications
  • Complete internships once accepted in good faith
  • Not misuse or abuse the platform
  • Not submit false or misleading information

4. Company Responsibilities

As a company user, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate company information and business registration details
  • Post genuine internship opportunities with accurate descriptions
  • Respond to student applications in a timely manner
  • Provide meaningful learning experiences for interns
  • Issue placement letters for accepted interns
  • Not discriminate against applicants based on race, gender, religion, or disability
  • Not use the platform for illegal or unethical hiring practices

5. Internship Placement Process

RIAPMS facilitates the connection between students and companies. You understand that:

  • We do not guarantee internship placement
  • Final hiring decisions rest with the company
  • We are not responsible for internship terms, compensation, or conditions
  • Both parties should communicate directly to clarify expectations
